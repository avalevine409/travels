The History Behind my Travels:

My family and I have traveled a lot within the past few years. This year however, was the first year I went without them. This year, I went on foreign exchange with my school to the Netherlands.

The Netherlands was an amazing experience. I went to the Netherlands with my foreign exchange program at my school. My friends and I got to experience the Dutch life and culture. We got to go to our host students school as well as bike around different cities. As for landmarks, I went to the Anne Frank House, the Rememberance Museum, and on a bike tour of the town I stayed in. I would totally recommend going to the Netherlands if you are ever going to Europe in the future.
